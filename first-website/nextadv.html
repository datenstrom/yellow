Mexico City - Spring 2019


This has always been a place that I have always wanted to visit! Well actually I'm lying. Sometimes I have a layover at the airport which is in the city. I have just never taken the occasion to explore.

There are so many awesome places to see! I do have a list of places I really want to see, check it out below! I can see it now... the monuments, the city squares, the churches and just the plain beauty of the different parts of the city.


I have many family members in the city that I have never met before. I've been to Mexico plenty of times but I've always stayed in the southern region. This is the time to explore a new place and indulge in another portion of my Mexican Heritage. I'm also just curious how my city relatives act compared to my southern relatives. Should I be worried..... Who am I kidding!
